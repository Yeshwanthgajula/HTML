QSpiders Lo Modalaaina Snehabandham

Once upon a time Hyderabad lo unna QSpiders Chaitanyapuri lo oka kottha batch start ayyindi Aa first day class ki naluguru abbayilu vachharu Yeshwanth, Shammi, Goutham, mariyu Puneeth. Modati roju andharu okariki okaru strangers. Class lo trainer oka activity cheyyamani cheppaga, vallandaru oka group ayyaru. Akkade vallaki first introduction jarigindi. Yeshwanth chala silent ga untadu kani avasaram ayithe andhariki help chesthadu. Shammi matram full comedy piece. Athani jokes valla class motham navvutho untundi. Goutham intelligent and calm. Evarikaina doubt unte easy ga explain chesthadu. Puneeth energetic and friendly. Andharini kalupukoni team spirit maintain chesthadu. Roju morning class ki kalisi vachhevaru. Attendance ayyaka back bench lo kalisi kurchoni classes vinnevaru. Break time lo tea taguthu future gurinchi matladukunevaru. SQL, Java, Aptitude practice chesthu okariki okaru support chesevaru. Exams daggara padithe night varaku preparation chesevaru. Okariki ardham kakapothe migatha mugguru explain chesevaru. Success ni kalisi celebrate chesevaru, failures vachhina okariki okaru dhairyam cheppevaru. Weekend vaste Hyderabad lo different places ki velladam, food tinadam, selfies thisukovadam vallaki oka habit ayyindi. Class lo andariki ee naluguru friendship chala famous ayyindi. Oka roju Shammi cheppadu, "Manam jobs vachhina tarvatha kuda ee friendship marchipokudadhu." Andharu navvuthu handshake chesi promise chesaru. Konni rojula tarvatha interviews start ayyayi. Okariki job vachhina andharu kalisi celebrate chesevaru. Endukante vallaki success ante okkari success kaadhu, andhari success. Ala QSpiders Chaitanyapuri lo modalaaina oka chinna parichayam, jeevithantham gurthundipoye oka goppa snehabandham ga maarindi. Moral: Nijamaina friends ante kashtallo support chesi, santhosham lo kalisi navve vallane.
